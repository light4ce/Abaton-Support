2. Service description

Abaton is an on-device privacy tool that provides:

• Encrypted storage for photos, videos, and recordings
• Encrypted notes

• Dual-vault privacy model
• Backup & device transfer (.enc filename, zip inside; optional iCloud container on iOS)

There is no cloud sync service; day-to-day data stays on your device unless you export or back up it yourself.
